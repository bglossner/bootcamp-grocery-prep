Turkey Sandwhich

The easiest, most reliable lunch you can imagine

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 3 turkey slices
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 4 scoops of mayo

Instructions

  1. Lather both slices of bread up with all the mayo
  2. Put some turkey and cheese on the mayo
