Guac

Another lovely food to pair with tacos or salsa.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 lime
  • 1 tbsp of salt

Instructions

  1. Mash avocados in a large bowl
  2. Stir in chopped tomato and onion
  3. Mince garlic and add to bowl
  4. Juice lime and add to bowl
  5. Add salt
  6. Chill in fridge for half an hour
  7. Serve with chips
