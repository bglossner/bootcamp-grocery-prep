Tuna Casserole

If you can get over your hatred of tuna, you will enjoy this one

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna
  • 1 box of mac and cheese
  • 1 can of cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 6 cheese slices

Instructions

  1. Cook mac and cheese as normal
  2. Mix in cream of mushroom soup and tuna
  3. Put in a casserole dish and top with cheese slices
  4. Bake in oven at 350 for 25 minutes
