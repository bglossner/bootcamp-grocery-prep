Brownies and Ice Cream

Desserts simply don't get better than this

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of brownie mix
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 pint of ice cream

Instructions

  1. Mix brownie mix, eggs, and vegetable oil
  2. Bake for 20 mins at 350
  3. Serve over ice cream
